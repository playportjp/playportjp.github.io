Product Description

Experience the unforgettable RPG that redefined the genre with this Japanese edition of Final Fantasy VII Remake for PlayStation 4. This version features the complete game experience with Japanese voice acting and text.

This pre-owned copy is in excellent condition. The disc shows minimal signs of use with no scratches that would affect gameplay. The original case, cover art, and manual are all included and in great condition.

Final Fantasy VII Remake is a reimagining of the iconic 1997 PlayStation game that redefined the RPG genre. This first game in the FFVII Remake project expands on the storyline of Midgar into a full standalone game.
